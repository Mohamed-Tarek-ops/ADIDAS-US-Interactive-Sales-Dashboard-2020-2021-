# 📊 Adidas US Interactive Sales Dashboard (2020-2021)

## 📝 Project Overview
This repository features a comprehensive, **interactive sales performance dashboard** built entirely in **Microsoft Excel**. The project analyzes Adidas's sales operations across the United States for the fiscal years 2020 and 2021. By transforming a raw dataset of over 9,000 transactions into a dynamic visual tool, this project provides actionable insights into regional performance, product profitability, and retail channel efficiency.

---

## 🎯 Key Business Metrics (KPIs)
The dashboard tracks four primary performance indicators to evaluate business health:
* **Total Sales:** Gross revenue generated across all retail partners.
* **Total Profit:** Net operating profit after costs.
* **Units Sold:** Total volume of inventory moved.
* **Operating Margin:** Percentage reflecting the efficiency of sales operations.

---

## 🛠️ Technical Implementation
To ensure a seamless user experience and data integrity, the following **Advanced Excel** techniques were utilized:

* **Data Transformation (Power Query):** Cleaned and normalized raw data, handled missing values, and standardized date/currency formats for consistency.
* **Dynamic Aggregation:** Leveraged **Pivot Tables** to categorize and summarize complex data points across multiple dimensions.
* **Interactive UI/UX:** Integrated **Slicers and Timelines**, allowing users to filter the entire dashboard by *Region, Retailer, Product Category, and Sales Method* with a single click.
* **Professional Visualization:** Designed custom charts (Clustered Bar, Line, and Doughnut charts) with a unified Adidas-themed color palette.

---

## 📈 Dashboard Insights & Analysis
* **Temporal Trends:** Identified seasonal peaks (e.g., Q3 spikes) to assist in inventory planning and marketing strategies.
* **Regional Dominance:** Comparative analysis of performance across the West, Northeast, Southeast, South, and Midwest regions.
* **Retailer Benchmarking:** Performance evaluation of key partners including **Foot Locker, Walmart, Sports Direct, and West Gear**.
* **Channel Optimization:** Comparison of **In-store, Online, and Outlet** sales to determine the most profitable distribution strategy.
